About This Property

Nestled along the shimmering shores of Biscayne Bay, this extraordinary waterfront villa redefines luxury coastal living. From the moment you arrive, the grand entry with soaring ceilings and floor-to-ceiling windows framing unobstructed bay views sets the tone for an exceptional residence.

The open-plan main level seamlessly connects a chef-caliber kitchen — outfitted with sub-zero appliances, custom cabinetry, and an oversized marble island — to a light-flooded living room and formal dining space. Sliding glass walls dissolve the boundary between indoor and outdoor entertaining, leading to an expansive wraparound terrace, a resort-style heated pool, and a private boat dock below.

The upper floor hosts four en-suite bedrooms including a sprawling primary suite with dual walk-in closets, a spa bathroom with a freestanding soaking tub, and a private balcony overlooking the water. Two additional guest suites occupy the ground level, ideal for staff or extended family. Smart home technology controls lighting, security, climate, and audio throughout the entire property.

Additional highlights include a private home cinema, a glass-enclosed wine cellar, a fitness room, and a rooftop observation deck with 360-degree panoramic views of the Miami skyline and bay. The three-car garage features epoxy flooring and EV charging stations. Located minutes from world-class dining, Brickell City Centre, and Miami International Airport, this property represents the pinnacle of South Florida waterfront living.
