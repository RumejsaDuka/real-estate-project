Who We Are

Dedicated to Finding Your Perfect Home

Grand Realty was founded in Los Angeles in 2009 with a singular vision: to bring honesty, expertise, and genuine care to every real estate transaction. What started as a small boutique agency has grown into one of the most trusted names in premium property across the country.

We specialize in luxury residential sales, investment properties, and first-time buyer guidance — offering a full-service experience from initial search to closing day and beyond.

Client-First Philosophy Every decision we make is guided by what's truly best for our clients.
Deep Market Knowledge Our agents deliver insights you won't find anywhere online.
End-to-End Support From pre-approval to moving day, we're with you every step.
